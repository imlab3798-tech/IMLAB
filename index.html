Prof. Sung Wook Baik

Full Professor | Director, Intelligent Media Laboratory

I am a Full Professor at the Department of Software and Director of Intelligent Media Laboratory (IM Lab), Sejong University, Seoul, Korea. Since 2002, I have served Sejong University in key leadership roles including Dean, Managing Director of National Program of Excellence in Software, and Vice President of Research Affairs.

My research interests include image processing, pattern recognition, video analytics, Big Data analysis, multimedia processing, IoT, IIoT, smart cities, and energy forecasting.
